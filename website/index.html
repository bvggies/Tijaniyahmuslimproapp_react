Frequently asked questions

Is the app free?

Yes. We plan to keep the core features free and ad-free.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Most features cache data and sync when you reconnect.

Which platforms are supported?

Android and iOS via Expo. A web app is planned.

How accurate are prayer times?

We support multiple calculation methods and fine-tuning; you can adjust to your local masjid‚Äôs timetable.

Can I change the app language?

Yes. Switch languages in settings; major screens update instantly via our i18n system.

Is my journal private?

Your journal is private to your account. Entries are securely stored; you can export or delete anytime.

How do Community posts persist?

Posts, comments, and likes are saved in our hosted backend (Postgres + API) so they survive app reinstalls.

Does Makkah Live need YouTube sign-in?

No. We use privacy-enhanced embeds and block sign-in redirects; you can also open directly in YouTube.

What permissions are required?

Location (for Qibla and nearby mosques) and notifications (for reminders). You can disable them anytime.

How can I report an issue?

Use the Contact section or our GitHub link to open an issue; we usually respond within 48 hours.
